The Story of my Life

I have been working since i was 19 years old. I was not able to finish my degree because i need to get a full time job to help my family. I started working in a call center as a customer service representative. I, too, worked as a technical support representative for a Laptop and ISP provider. I got promoted as a Quality Analyst for a healthcare account before i became an Email Marketing Analyst for a company based in the U.S. I have been working from home for 6 years now. I have started getting full and part time jobs and i was able to manage my time properly.
